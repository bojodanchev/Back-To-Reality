# Back To Reality

A transformative program that helps aspiring entrepreneurs build real businesses using AI technology and structured mentorship.

## About

Back to Reality is a comprehensive entrepreneurship program that combines:
- AI-powered business tools (16 specialized AI assistants)
- Structured learning system ("The Creator's Formula")
- Real-world mentorship and daily support
- Community of creators and entrepreneurs
- Opportunity for funding up to 50,000 BGN

## Program Structure

### The Creator's Formula - 3 Core Pillars:

1. **Cognitive Clarity (Mind Clarity)**
   - Clear direction and niche selection
   - Market analysis aligned with personal strengths
   - Strategic planning and goal setting

2. **Intelligent Execution (AI Execution)**
   - 16 AI assistants handling routine tasks
   - Focus on growth rather than mechanical work
   - Automated content, marketing, and business processes

3. **Realization Environment (Social Realization)**
   - Supportive community of creators
   - Reality show format for visibility
   - Funding opportunities through "The AI Arena"

## 7-Stage Journey

1. **Mind & Body Control** - Energy, focus, and daily routines
2. **Mindset & Discipline** - Building sustainable habits
3. **Business Plan** - 30/90/180 day goals with clear actions
4. **Skills Development** - Learning income-generating abilities
5. **Client Acquisition** - Real sales and revenue generation
6. **Team Building** - Scaling through partnerships
7. **Automation** - Systems and long-term growth

## Features

- 8-week intensive program
- Daily tasks and feedback
- Access to 16 specialized AI bots
- Live business building demonstrations
- Weekly deep-dive sessions
- Private community access
- Priority access to "Back to Reality Show" and "The AI Arena"

## The Shows

### Back to Reality Show
A documentary-style reality show following entrepreneurs through the entire journey from idea to execution.

### The AI Arena
A pitch competition where participants present their AI-powered businesses to investors for funding opportunities up to 50,000 BGN monthly.

## Target Audience

Perfect for people who:
- Want to start their first business from scratch
- Want to work remotely using just a phone
- Are willing to take action and build discipline
- Know that success requires a system and support
- Are ready to act, not just dream

- Monthly membership: 100 BGN
- Includes all program materials, AI tools, and community access

## Guarantee

If you follow the system for 30 days and don't see measurable progress, receive double your money back.

## Vision

Making entrepreneurship accessible to everyone through AI technology, structured systems, and real support. Empowering the next generation of Bulgarian creators and business builders.
